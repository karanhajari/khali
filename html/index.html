Appointment Managment System

Connects and integrates all your appointment channels – Website, Leads, Mobile, and front desk. Everything is in a central calendar! Set up appointments for your patients in the most effective way with the help of the Appointment Scheduling App. The doctors will be able to access, reception and appointments, everything in one calendar making it more simplified and organized. This is a tailor-made software for Clinics, Hospitals and Doctors allowing a smooth flow in the operations, thereby replacing the manual work with ease and comfort through the medical software.
